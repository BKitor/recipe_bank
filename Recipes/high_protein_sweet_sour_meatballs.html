High-Protein Sweet & Sour Meatballs (Meal Prep)

Yield: ~5 meal prep servings
Protein: ~45–50 g per serving (with rice)
Total time: ~45 minutes

Ingredients

Meatballs

  • 2+ lb lean ground turkey(93–99%)/pork mix
  • 1 egg
  • ½ cup panko breadcrumbs (or oat flour)
  • ½ cup finely minced onion
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil

Sweet & Sour Sauce

  • 1 cup pineapple juice (or blended pineapple chunks)
  • ¼ cup rice vinegar
  • ¼ cup ketchup
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar or honey (optional)
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1 scoop unflavored collagen or whey isolate (optional protein boost)
  • ½ cup water

Vegetables

  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks
  • 1 medium onion, chopped

For Serving

  • 4–5 cups cooked jasmine or brown rice

Instructions

1. Make the Meatballs

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. In a large bowl mix ground turkey, egg whites, breadcrumbs, onion, garlic, soy sauce, ginger, sesame oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Form 30–35 meatballs (~1.5 tbsp each).
  4. Place on a parchment-lined baking sheet.
  5. Bake 15–18 minutes until cooked through.

2. Make the Sauce

Whisk together pineapple juice, rice vinegar, ketchup, soy sauce, brown sugar, water, cornstarch, and optional protein powder.

3. Cook the Vegetables

  1. Heat a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add a small amount of oil.
  3. Stir-fry onion, bell peppers, and pineapple for 3–4 minutes until slightly softened.

4. Combine

  1. Add baked meatballs to the pan.
  2. Pour sauce over everything.
  3. Simmer 3–5 minutes until thickened and glossy.

Meal Prep Assembly

  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 6–7 meatballs
  • 1 cup sweet & sour vegetables

Keeps 4–5 days refrigerated. Freezes well for 2–3 months.
